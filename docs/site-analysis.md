# MediMan Live Site Analysis

**Source:** https://mediman.life/
**Date:** 2026-02-14

## 1. Brand Identity

- **Primary Pink:** `#CC0052`
- **Primary Blue:** `#1751C2`
- **Alternate Purple:** `#7E57C2`
- **Background:** Clean white with light grey section dividers.

## 2. Page Structure

The site is a clean, single-page-style marketing site with a separate doctors application.

### Header

- **Logo:** SVG Text/Icon
- **Nav:** Home, Doctor, Available Doctors, FAQs, Contact
- **CTA:** "Download" (Links to App Store/Play Store)

### Hero Section

- **Headline:** "Healthcare. Anytime. Anywhere."
- **Subtext:** "Find your favourite doctors, get e-prescriptions, manage appointments and followups, and keep your medical records securely in one app."
- **Visual:** Mobile app mockup showing the interface.
- **CTAs:** Google Play & App Store buttons.

### Key Features

1.  **Book in Minutes:** "Skip the waiting room. Book online or in-clinic appointments instantly."
2.  **Digital Records:** "Prescriptions, lab reports, and past visits all in one place."
3.  **Family Health:** "Manage profiles for your parents, spouse, and children."

### Doctor App (Secondary Pitch)

- Targeted at doctors to join the platform.
- **Value Prop:** "manage both online and in-person appointments, issue e-prescriptions... and receive payouts."

### Footer

- **Contact:** `+94 11 466 8668` | `support@mediman.life`
- **Socials:** Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube.
- **Links:** Privacy Policy, Terms, FAQs.
